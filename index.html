Room types

single occupancy room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of Single Rooms is generally between 37 m² to 45 m².

family room

A room assigned to four people. May have two or more beds. The room size or area of Quad Rooms is generally between 70 m² to 85 m².

duplex room

This type of room is always adjoining to the swimming pool or has a private pool attached to the room. The room size or area of Cabana Room Types is generally between 30 m² to 45 m².
